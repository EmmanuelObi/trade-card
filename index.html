Eagle

"An eagle's flight is not just its wings beating; it's a symphony of determination, grace, and unyielding freedom soaring across the boundless sky.""

The eagle, with its majestic wingspan and piercing gaze, epitomizes strength, agility, and untamed wilderness.

  • Eye Color: Brown or Yellow
  • Diet: Eagles are carnivorous predators, primarily feeding on fish, small mammals, birds, and occasionally carrion.

Eagles serve vital roles in conservation, education, and symbolism. They maintain ecological balance, symbolize strength and freedom, and attract tourists for wildlife viewing. Additionally, studying eagles aids scientific understanding and conservation efforts.
